In the name of Allah, the entirely merciful, the especially merciful. Let me begin with the sincerest intention all for the sake of truth. This page is a compilation of my work on the thesis: the bible says Jesus (A.S.) is God. The content contains bible verses and questions related to the doctrine of trinity.

The life of Jesus (A.S.) plays a pivotal role to both Christianity and Islam. The two religions believe Jesus (A.S.) as the Messiah sent by Allah (God) to the children of Israel. His divinity is the core belief of the majority of the Christian denominations around the world, but is rejected by the Qur'an (the holy book of Islam). So the main question is, is Jesus (A.S.) God?
